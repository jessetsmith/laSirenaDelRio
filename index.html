About La Sirena

Naomy is a 22 year old flight-attendant, and avid beach-goer.
Growing up in the sexy city of Miami, she spent much of her life running on beaches, and swimming in the emerald waters. Which meant that bikinis and swimsuits were a major part of her wardrobe. Durability was a must, but so is looking good. La Sirena del Rio's swimwear comes straight from Brazil, where the quality is renowned. Our bikinis and one-pieces will not only keep up with your lifestyle, but they will keep you looking and feeling as sexy as you are supposed to.
